About Us

JW Jet Washing offers a comprehensive driveway cleaning service designed to restore the appearance and longevity of your driveway using industry-leading techniques and materials. Our team is reliable, fully insured, and dedicated to providing high-quality results with attention to detail.

What We Do

Driveway Cleaning – Full Service Package

Surface Preparation & Cleaning: Removal of dirt, moss, and surface debris to ensure optimal cleaning results.

Application of Professional Cleaning Solutions: Eco-friendly chemical agents applied to break down tough stains and biological growth.

High-Pressure Jet Washing: Full jet wash to lift and remove deep-seated grime and residue.

Optional Re-sanding (for block paving): Reapplication of kiln-dried sand between blocks to maintain structure and prevent shifting.

Pricing: £3.50 per square meter (Custom quotes available for larger or more complex areas)
